Design Principles

White Space, Hick's Law, and Visual Hierarchy

White Space

  • Apple
  • https://www.apple.com/

I chose Apple.com for white space because I liked how the empty space was used to separate the different elements.

You can particularly see this when they highlight each product. The design helps to divide the products while organizing the information. Its a satisfying presentaion.

The Rule of Thirds

  • Nike
  • https://www.nike.com

Visual Hierarchy helps guide the user to what is most important. It leads the consumer through the process of decision making and purchaing.

Nike does a good job with this. You can easily follow the steps as you see the main attraction, the product, color selection, shoe size, etc. .

Hick's Law

  • Linked In
  • https://www.linkedin.com/

Hick's Law is about making choosing easier, at least for good design it is. I felt linked In did a great job making the choice easy. You have just four choices: Sign In, find a job, find a person, or learn a skill.

All these things are pertinent to anyone who is looking for employment or to better their career.
