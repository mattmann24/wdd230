Membership Program

We are pleased to present you with our Chamber of Commerce Membership Program, designed to provide value and benefits tailored to your organization's needs. Our program offers four tiers: Non-Profit, Bronze, Silver, and Gold. Each level comes with increasing costs and a range of benefits to help your business thrive. Here's an overview of what each tier offers:

Non-Profit

Cost: Free Access
  • Access to networking events and business mixers
  • Listing in the Chamber's online directory
  • Opportunities to collaborate with other non-profit organizations
  • Discounted rates for Chamber-sponsored training programs

Bronze

Cost: $100 Annually
  • Exclusive invitations to Chamber luncheons and seminars
  • Spotlight position on the Chamber's home page for one month per year
  • Complimentary booth space at select Chamber events
  • Access to member-only resources and business tools

Silver

Cost: $250 Annually
  • Priority registration and discounted rates for Chamber-sponsored training programs
  • Enhanced advertising opportunities on the Chamber's website and social media platforms
  • Recognition as a Silver Member in Chamber publications
  • Access to industry-specific workshops and roundtable discussions

Gold

Cost: $400 Annually
  • VIP access to exclusive Chamber events and high-level networking opportunities
  • Featured spotlight position on the Chamber's home page for two months per year
  • Customized promotional opportunities in Chamber newsletters and email campaigns
  • Recognition as a Gold Member in Chamber press releases and promotional materials
